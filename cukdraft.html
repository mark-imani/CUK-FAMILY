About CUK Family

We are a dynamic and visionary student-based movement and a club at Co-operative University that annually onboards 500 first-year students drawn from all schools in the university. Our core mandate is to spark disruptive innovation, bridge the gap between theory and real-world experience, and secure rare opportunities for our members across Mt kenya region,the country and East Africa’s leading organizations and industry hubs. MUST Family is more than an club — it’s a transformation hub tailored to propel ambitious scholars into distinction.

Tailored Opportunities for CUK Family Members

At CUK Family, we believe that every student deserves more than just a degree — they deserve an advantage. Our opportunities are designed to give you a competitive edge in the job market, access to rare networks, and exposure to real-world experience. Whether you're in **SCCD**, **SBE**, **SCM**, or pursuing a **Diploma or Certificate program**, there’s a door of opportunity waiting for you.

SCCD – School of Cooperative and Community Development

Degree Programs: Bachelor of Co-operative Business, Disaster Risk Management, Community Development, Agribusiness, Environmental Economics, Counseling Psychology, Development Studies, etc.

Opportunities Include:

  • 🌍 Partner Organizations: Co-operative Bank of Kenya, SASRA, National Co-operative Housing Union, Red Cross, UNDP, FAO, World Bank-funded projects.
  • 🏆 Special Programs:
    • Co-operative Leadership & Governance Bootcamps
    • Agribusiness Innovation Incubators (KALRO & AFA Youth Programs)
    • Disaster Management Simulations with Kenya Red Cross
  • 📜 Certifications: Certified Co-operative Manager (CCM), Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), Disaster Risk Management (UNISDR).
  • 🌐 Global Exposure: International internships with NGOs, scholarships via Co-operative Alliance International.

SBE – School of Business and Economics

📌 CUK Family Quick Facts

What is CUK Family? A united student community and a club.

Core function?

  • Linking the members to outside rare opportunities
  • Securing internships and attachments for the members
  • Mentorship forums for different career paths
  • Innovation and invention

Who should join? All CUK students — but only the first 500 first-year students are accepted.

When to join? New membership is done once, when you are a first-year student.

How to join? Through the registration form below.

Is there a membership fee? Yes — KES 500 only.
